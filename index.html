IB Students and Stimulant Use

Feb 27, 2023
If you are an IB student, chances are you have a secret stash of caffeine pills, energy drinks, or even stronger substances to help you survive the rigorous curriculum. A recent study by researchers at the University of Oxford has revealed that a staggering 99% of IB students are addicted to stimulants for staying awake. The study surveyed over 10,000 IB students from around the world and asked them about their use of stimulants such as caffeine, nicotine, modafinil, Adderall, and Ritalin.…
